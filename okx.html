OKX Company Overview

OKX encourages its users to discover the world of crypto built on top of the world's most powerful exchange. OKX (previously referred to as OKEx) is one of the industry's top leaders in cryptocurrency trading instruments and pairs for traders worldwide. The company has over 20 million traders in over 200 countries and is the most trusted cryptocurrency marketplace in the world. You can get digital assets BTC, XRP, ETH, and USDT. You can use payment methods such as Visa and Mastercard to find the lowest prices and more efficient conversions. Therefore, the company prides itself in ensuring users can trade crypto more effectively with several crypto pairs, derivative trading instruments, and order type limits such as limit order, market order, stop order, and iceberg orders. Users should also be able to use OKX to diversify their crypto portfolios to create long-term wealth.

Problems facing OKX

A fundamental problem that OKX presented to us is the oracle dilemma that Blockchain faces. An oracle acts as the bridge between the blockchain and the real world. Therefore, the OKX platform is based on creating connections between smart contracts and the outside world, and it is not a physical medium. OKX is facing a dilemma with feeding information from the real world and vice versa.


OKX presented that they are facing some challenges with introducing external data into the blockchain as it is a close-looped system, for example, in the NFT marketplace. The concept of blockchain is the security of your data and the close-looped nature. OKX risks losing hundreds of million dollars due to their designs of Oracle and necessary cross-chain bridges. Therefore, OKX wanted to partner with us to prevent the probability of a massive DeFi theft that could spur losses and brand damage, especially in the new NFT field.

Solutions provided by us

Our solution for OKX focuses on their vulnerability to bugs while guarding their NFT platform. Malicious players focus on these bugs as most organizations rely on conventional measures of vulnerability management. These measures are based on a framework that first mends the highest severity vulnerabilities. We suggested that this platform, the Common Vulnerability Scoring System isn't the best system for cryptocurrency platforms.


As a result of the issue, we suggested to OKX that they adopt an alternative measuring system that focuses on less critical vulnerabilities.

Technology stack used

We advised OKX that the best and most secure technology stack to use is Supra Oracles. They have acknowledged this issue and proceeded to implement practical solutions, especially for NFT marketplaces. It is Blockchain's best, fastest, most secure, and most accurate off-chain data.
