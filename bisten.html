Bitsten company overview

One of the most valuable platforms for cryptocurrency exchange is Bitsten. The company was launched in Indonesia in 2017 as we shared a vision with its founders. Bitsten and Buster apps collaborate to ensure Bitsten offers the most advanced cryptocurrency exchange platform. This platform is based on the ease of use due to its simple user experience features while trading.


Bitsten believes in improving the crypto trading experience for every user. As a result, Bitsten encourages you to join them so as to have a worthwhile cryptocurrency exchange experience which the platform guarantees. The platform has formed a global community of cryptocurrency investors who are able to experience fast and easy conversions between assets.

Problem facing Bitsten

The company approached us at Buster apps with a potential problem that could affect its future. While Bitsten has already created a global community, the team asserted to us that they could grow it further and further from where it currently is. However, they are facing hindrances due to the anti-government/ anarchy mindset. They are afraid of government power which we at Buster apps identified as their main problem. The government has excessive power and has often proven to make decisions based on fear thereby leading to more military, police enforcement, and population control. While the government plays a critical role in finance and economics, it can also jeopardize innovative ideas due to the lack of this control. The dilemma is that Bitsten is based on the decentralization of virtually all its functions.


The solution to the problem

Bitsten can lead the efforts to create safe and secure platforms which the government supports. Its main issue is a lack of control. And while cryptocurrency cannot negotiate this aspect, creating legitimate platforms can help counter issues or corruption, among others.


Therefore, our recommendation is that Bitsten creates a platform whose core values are based on solving the problems society faces that are under the government's jurisdiction.

Technology stack used

We at Buster apps chose C++ as the main technological stack. This is due to the following reasons; mental memory management, a high level of optimization, and the stack have been in the game for so long that most governments would be inclined to trust platforms based on the stack.
